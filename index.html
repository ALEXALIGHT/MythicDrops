MythicDrops

Action RPG style drops in Spigot

Get Started

Randomized Items

With over 52 billion items able to be generated from the default settings, MythicDrops can power your RPG Minecraft server with any items you can imagine.

Socket Gems

An action RPG favorite, Socket Gems can add effects to your items. Apply blindness on hit, confuse your enemies when hit. The possibilities are endless.

Unidentified Items

Items can drop as Unidentified Items that need to be identified in order to gain value. Is the drop you just got from that Zombie a common drop or is it a Legendary?
